### Q. How to view metrics?

<details>

<summary> Toggle for answer </summary>

You can explore all ingested metrics in the Metrics Explorer.

Or you can also [Add a panel](https://signoz.io/docs/userguide/manage-panels/) in a Dashboard to view the metrics in dropdown. 

</details>
